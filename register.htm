To apply for a place on the Durham school please complete the form below. The cost of the course is £900, which includes registration, accomodation, all meals for the whole course and a full set of lecture notes. We hope to be able to offer a limited number of bursaries for both UK and overseas applicants. Please indicate in the registration form if you wish to apply for one of these bursaries.

Places are limited and assigned on a first-come-first-serve basis. However, and in case of over-subscription, the organisers may have to prioritise applications (e.g. maximum number of attendees per institution/group, to maintain gender balance in line with that currently found more widely in science). Submission of a registration form does not guarantee your place on the course. Places will be confirmed by e-mail soon after your registration. If for any reason you wish to withdraw your application please contact h.a.sparkes@durham.ac.uk as soon as possible.

The number of places on the course is strictly limited, SO APPLY AS SOON AS POSSIBLE.

The deadline for application is 9th January 2023.

All final payments are due by 15th February 2019.
